The Romantic Thread

Love letters for a soulmate I am still writing toward.

I am a hopeless romantic. Part of this studio is art, part of it is story, and part of it is a book of love letters reaching for the person who feels like home before I know the address.

Dear you, I keep finding blue in the places where longing learns to breathe. If I could send a room instead of a sentence, it would sound like rain and look like this. Somewhere between ink and memory, I am still becoming the person who can meet you clearly.
