Pixels Pricing

Suggested print pricing for Pixels.com.

Use this as a markup guide when you are inside your Pixels artist account. Keep entry prints affordable, then let larger sizes and premium surfaces carry stronger profit.

Product Type Suggested Artist Markup Customer Price Goal
Small paper prints / posters $8-$15 Impulse-friendly entry point around $25-$45 total.
Medium framed / canvas prints $20-$45 Core buyer range around $75-$160 total.
Large statement prints $50-$125 Premium wall-art range around $180-$400+ total.
Metal / acrylic / specialty surfaces $60-$150 Use for bold blue pieces and signature works.
Home goods / phone cases / accessories $3-$12 Lower markup, higher impulse potential.

Recommendation: choose 3-5 strongest artworks first, set consistent markups, then adjust after you see which sizes and products people actually click or buy.
