Tell the studio what you need.

Use this path for commissions, print questions, brush purchases, collaboration ideas, custom web rooms, or general contact. It works on GitHub Pages, keeps a local outbox, preserves email fallback, and can post to a Slack relay when configured.

To send directly into Slack, point window.INKSPIRATIONS_LEAD_RELAY_URL or localStorage key inkspirationsLeadRelayUrl at a private relay such as Pipedream, Make, Zapier, or a small serverless function. Do not publish a raw Slack webhook URL in this repo.
