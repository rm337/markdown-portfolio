# Robert's AI Thinking Principle

**Author:** Robert Marleton  
**Studio:** Inkspirations Studios  
**Status:** Saved creative principle  
**Date recorded:** 2026-07-24

## Original Thought

> I don't necessarily know how to create a prompt, but I know how to think well enough to know what to ask AI to write.

## Refined Versions

### Direct
> I may not always know how to write the perfect prompt, but I know how to think through what I need and ask AI to help shape it.

### Professional
> Prompt writing is not the same as creative thinking. My strength is recognizing the right problem, asking the right questions, and directing AI toward a useful result.

### Strongest Positioning Statement
> I do not need to begin with a polished prompt. I begin with an idea, a problem, or a goal. I know what to question, what to challenge, and what the result should accomplish. AI helps me translate that thinking into the prompt.

### Inkspirations Studios Principle
> Robert provides the thinking, intent, judgment, and creative direction. AI helps structure the prompt and execute the work. AI assists. Robert decides.

## Why It Matters

This is not a weakness. It describes a higher-value skill than merely knowing prompt formulas:

- Identifying the real problem
- Knowing what outcome is needed
- Asking useful follow-up questions
- Recognizing when an answer is incomplete or wrong
- Directing revisions
- Applying creative judgment
- Making the final decision

## Possible LinkedIn Post Angle

Do not frame this as “I don't know how to prompt.” Frame it as a distinction between prompt mechanics and human direction:

> People talk a lot about writing the perfect AI prompt. I have learned that the more important skill is knowing what to ask, why it matters, and what a good result should accomplish.
>
> I do not always begin with polished prompt language. I begin with an idea, a problem, or a goal. Then I use AI to help structure the request, test possibilities, and refine the result.
>
> The value is not only in typing the prompt. It is in the thinking, judgment, and creative direction behind it.
>
> AI assists. I decide.
