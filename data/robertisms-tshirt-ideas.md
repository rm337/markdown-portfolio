## T Time

**Category:** Satire / Wordplay / T-Shirt Concept  
**Status:** Archived concept

A large elegant script **T** with a sloping or wavy line that functions as both a hill and rising steam.

**Layered meanings:**
- Tea time
- Testosterone, or “T”
- King of the Hill

The visual humor comes from refined tea-brand styling colliding with testosterone satire.

## Promptness

**Category:** AI Humor / Robertism / T-Shirt Concept  
**Status:** Seed

Wordplay based on **prompt** meaning both a quick response and an instruction given to AI.

**Core phrases:**
- Thanks for the prompt reply.
- I really appreciate their promptness with their work.
- I appreciate your promptness.

The joke works in ordinary conversation while carrying a second meaning for people who use AI.

## Get Over Here, Prompto!

**Category:** AI Humor / Robertism / T-Shirt Concept  
**Status:** Seed

A playful AI-flavored twist on **“Get over here, pronto!”**

**Front idea:** GET OVER HERE, PROMPTO!  
**Back idea:** Good instructions get results.

## Prompt and to the Point

**Category:** AI Humor / Robertism / T-Shirt Concept  
**Status:** Seed

A clean double meaning: concise communication and an effective AI prompt.

**Front idea:** PROMPT AND TO THE POINT.  
**Optional small line:** Better input. Better output.
